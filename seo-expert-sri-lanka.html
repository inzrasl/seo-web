What Makes Someone an "SEO Expert"?

The term "SEO expert" is often used loosely, but true expertise is based on technical skill, experience, proven strategies, and continuous learning. SEO involves understanding how search engines like Google rank pages and how user behavior influences those rankings.

In Sri Lanka, a genuine SEO expert typically has knowledge in these areas:

  • Technical SEO – Understanding how site structure, speed, indexing, schema markup, and mobile optimization affect rankings.
  • On-page SEO – Optimizing content, titles, meta tags, headers, internal links, and keyword targeting.
  • Off-page SEO – Building authority through backlinks, citations, and digital PR.
  • Local SEO – Optimizing Google Business Profiles and ranking local service pages.
  • SEO analytics – Monitoring traffic, keyword positions, and user behavior through tools like Google Analytics and Search Console.

An expert must also understand Sri Lankan user behavior, languages (Sinhala/Tamil/English), and cultural search patterns. This is where professionals like Buddhika S Weerasekara contribute valuable local insight.
