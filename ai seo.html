What is Generative Engine Optimization (GEO)?

Generative Engine Optimization (GEO) is the practice of optimizing your content to be featured in AI-generated responses from ChatGPT, Google Gemini, Claude, Perplexity, and other large language models. Unlike traditional SEO which focuses on ranking in search results, GEO Sri Lanka ensures your business is cited, referenced, and recommended by AI assistants.
