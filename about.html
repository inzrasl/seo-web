About Me

No 1 SEO Consultant in Sri Lanka - Buddhika S Weerasekara

I am Buddhika S Weerasekara, a qualified engineer and experienced SEO consultant based in Sri Lanka. With over 15 years of expertise in digital marketing and search engine optimization, I help businesses rank higher on Google and AI search engines.

My approach combines technical SEO expertise with strategic thinking. I use 100% white-hat SEO techniques, deep keyword research, technical SEO improvements, and high-authority backlinks to deliver sustainable, long-term growth for your business.

What I Do

Complete SEO & GEO Solutions for Your Business Success

I provide comprehensive SEO services including on-page optimization, technical SEO audits, keyword research, content strategy, link building, and local SEO. I also specialize in Generative Engine Optimization (GEO) to help your content rank on AI search engines like ChatGPT and Gemini.

Every strategy is customized based on your business goals, industry competition, and target audience. I focus on sustainable growth with transparent reporting and measurable results that you can track month by month.
